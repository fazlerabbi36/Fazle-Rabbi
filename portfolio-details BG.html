Background

The background refers to the part of an image that is behind the main subject or object. It plays a significant role in setting the context, depth, and overall feel of the image. A well-chosen background helps highlight the subject, ensuring it stands out, while still complementing the overall composition.

A background can be:
* A natural setting, cityscape, or a specific location.
* A pattern or texture.
* Or simply a gradient or solid color background.
In photo editing, the background can be removed, replaced, or blurred to enhance the focus on the subject, making it more prominent and clear in the image.
